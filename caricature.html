Caricature Studio

Caricature Studio is a vibe-coding experiment — a tiny generative app built end to end with AI as a creative collaborator.

Pick an adjective, an animal, and a signature accessory, and it draws a refined single-line caricature on the fly. Every illustration is generated procedurally from hand-built vector layers — no image files, no server, no AI calls at runtime — just a little drawing engine that composes an animal, an expression, and an accessory into one continuous line.
