About

UX Designer
& Leader

I'm a UX designer and leader with 15+ years turning complex, fragmented problems into clear, human experiences — across enterprise SaaS, developer tooling, and consumer products used by millions.

Along the way I've shaped design at companies like Atlassian, GoPro, and Adobe — from enterprise admin experiences and design systems to consumer platforms and the communities around them. I pair product strategy with hands-on craft, and I care as much about the systems and teams behind the work as the pixels in front of it.

Lately I've been focused on AI-powered design workflows — building with the latest tools, finding where they create real leverage, and exploring how they reshape what a designer can do.
